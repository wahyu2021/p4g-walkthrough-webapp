# Today's Schedule Widget

**Status:** Planned
**Priority:** High

## Description
This track focuses on building a "Today's Schedule" widget. The widget will act as a quick-reference guide that reads the user's progress via `ProgressContext` and automatically displays the content of the *first uncompleted day*. It ensures players always know their immediate next objective without having to scroll through the full timeline.
