# Social Stats & Collection Tracker

**Status:** Planned
**Priority:** Medium

## Description
A comprehensive tracking system for the player's Social Stats (Knowledge, Courage, Diligence, Understanding, Expression), Books read, and Quests completed. This allows players to manually keep their in-game state synced with the app, providing a better sense of overall completion alongside the daily schedule.
